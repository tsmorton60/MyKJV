          Search Tips

          Enter Bible word, words, or phrase (in quotes) to search.

          Wildcard = *
          right* will also find righteous, rightly, etc.
          Any Word = |
          Searches for ANY entered word.  i.e. day | night. ALL words is default.
          Limit Range = >
          Limits search to entered book(s). i.e. ot > holy; Psa > peace; Mat-Joh > mercy, etc.
          Search Order = ^
          Only matches in listed order, i.e. holy ^ lord.
